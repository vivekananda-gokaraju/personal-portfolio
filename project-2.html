Omega Aqua Terra Ad Campaign

Brand Identity and Poster Design

Project Overview

For this project, I created a poster for the Omega Aqua Terra 150M Co-Axial Master Chronometer watch. The main focus was to study the brand’s visual identity, including its fonts, color palette, layout, and overall design style, and apply those elements to my own poster. The goal was to make the design feel cohesive with the brand’s existing materials, as if the brand itself had created it, while practicing consistency and attention to detail in design.

Goals and Objectives

  • Understand how brands use design to communicate their identity and values.
  • Replicate brand-specific typography, colors, and layout in an original design.
  • Develop skills in creating materials that feel professionally aligned with a brand’s visual language.
  • Experiment with composition and hierarchy to create a clear and engaging poster layout.
